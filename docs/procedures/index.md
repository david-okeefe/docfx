# Procedures

## What are Procedures?
A Procedure is a written document detailing *specific* steps to follow when completing a task.  Steps within a Procedure can link to other Process or Procedure documents.  Procedures commonly include an overview, prerequisites, steps to follow, and references.  Procedures differ from Processes in their detailed level of granularity.
